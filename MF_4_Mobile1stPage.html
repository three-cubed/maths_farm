Maths Farm 4 Mobile

Maths Farm Mobile was designed so you can play Maths Farm on a mobile phone.
Maths Farm Mobile may also be played on computers as a simplified version of Maths Farm Classic.

On a mobile phone, use your phone in a portrait orientation.

Note that most mobile phones do not accomodate the drag-and-drop functionality offered by Maths Farm 4.


Features of Maths Farm Mobile:
A shorter time per play.
Simplified user interface for reduced number of buttons & display items.
Buttons & display items placed higher on the screen for when the keyboard is displayed on the lower part.
